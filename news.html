Galatasaray 1-3 Bayern München

Galatasaray hosted Bayern München in the third match of the UEFA Champions League Group A. The game played at the Ali Sami Yen Spor Kompleksi, ended with Bayern Munich winning 3-1. In the 8th minute of the match, Bayern München took the lead with a goal from Kingsley Coman. Galatasaray equalized with a penalty goal by Mauro Icardi in the 30th minute. Bayern München extended their lead in the 73rd minute with a goal from Kane and in the 79th minute with a goal from Musiala, ultimately winning the match 3-1. The Lions will travel to face Rizespor on Saturday, October 28th

Zaha signs for Galatasaray!

Galatasaray SK announced that it has signed Ivorian star Wilfried Zaha to a contract through the 2025-2026 Super League season. Zaha advanced into the Crystal Palace first team from their academy in 2010. In his three full seasons at Selhurst Park, he scored 18 goals in all competitions. In January 2013, he was transferred to Premier League giant Manchester United, when Manu legend manager Alex Ferguson’s last season at his club. Zaha remained loan at Palace until end of the season and helping them return to the EPL.
